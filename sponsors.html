AcYut is an ambitious project in the rapidly expanding field of humanoid robotics. Developed completely by the students of BITS Pilani, AcYut is India's first Indigenously Developed Humanoid Robot. The team consists of some of the best brains of the country working on the humanoid.


AcYut has been widely covered by national and international media.
